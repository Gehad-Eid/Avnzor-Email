Avnzor feedback email (CRM): merge fields and copy (English / Arabic)

Reference for implementing avnzor-feedback-email-CRM.html. Replace each {{FIELD_NAME}} in the HTML with your platform’s merge syntax, or with the values below for static sends.

Survey link: {{CTA_URL}} must be the real feedback/survey URL (often the same in both languages if the form is bilingual). Set this per campaign or per contact, depending on your tool.

1. Document & language

Use these together so the email renders LTR in English and RTL in Arabic.

Field (placeholder) English Arabic
{{HTML_LANG}}enar
{{HTML_DIR}}ltrrtl
{{BODY_DIR}}ltrrtl
{{BODY_LANG}}enar
{{WRAPPER_LANG}}enar
{{WRAPPER_DIR}}ltrrtl
{{CONTENT_LANG}}enar
